Does Your Vehicle Have ADAS?

Most modern vehicles already have Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS).

Answer these quick questions:

✔ Does your car have lane assist or steering assistance?

✔ Does your vehicle automatically brake to avoid collisions?

✔ Do you see blind spot warning lights in your mirrors?

✔ Does your dashboard show speed limit or road sign information?

✔ Does your car warn you if you get too close to another vehicle?

If you answered YES to any of these

Your vehicle likely has an ADAS safety system.

These systems rely on cameras, radar sensors and steering sensors that must be precisely calibrated after certain repairs.

Calibration may be needed after:

  • Windscreen replacement
  • Wheel alignment
  • Suspension repairs
  • Steering repairs
  • Accident or bumper repairs
  • Steering Angle Sensor reset
