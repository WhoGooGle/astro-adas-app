Where are ADAS sensors located?

Common ADAS sensor locations

Front radar (grille or badge) Windscreen camera Rear camera Blind spot radar Parking sensors Steering angle sensor

Why calibration is required

  • Windscreen replacement moves the camera position
  • Wheel alignment changes steering angle sensor reference
  • Accident repairs can move radar sensors
  • Suspension repairs change vehicle ride height

ADAS sensors require millimetre precision to operate correctly.
