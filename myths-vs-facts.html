ADAS Myths vs Facts

Myth: ADAS never needs calibration

Fact: Sensors and cameras must be precisely aligned and often require recalibration after repairs.

Myth: Calibration is only needed after accidents

Fact: Windscreen replacement, suspension repairs and wheel alignment can also require calibration.

Myth: If there is no warning light, everything is fine

Fact: Systems can be slightly misaligned without triggering a warning light.

Myth: ADAS replaces the driver

Fact: ADAS assists the driver but does not replace safe driving practices.
