Where are ADAS sensors located?

Front Windscreen Camera

Usually mounted behind the rear-view mirror.

Used for lane assist, traffic sign recognition and emergency braking.

Front Radar Sensor

Often located in the grille or behind the vehicle badge.

Used for adaptive cruise control and collision detection.

Side Radar Sensors

Located in the rear bumper or rear quarter panels.

Used for blind spot monitoring and rear cross traffic alert.

Rear Camera

Usually mounted near the number plate or boot handle.

Used for reverse camera and parking assist.

Steering Angle Sensor (SAS)

Located within the steering system.

Used to determine steering direction for stability control and ADAS systems.
