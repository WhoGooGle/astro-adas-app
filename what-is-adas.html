What is ADAS?

ADAS stands for Advanced Driver Assistance Systems.

These systems use cameras, radar sensors, and electronic steering sensors to help improve vehicle safety.

Common ADAS features include

  • Lane Keep Assist (LKA)
  • Lane Departure Warning (LDW)
  • Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB)
  • Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)
  • Blind Spot Monitoring (BSM)
  • Forward Collision Warning (FCW)
  • Traffic Sign Recognition
  • Rear Cross Traffic Alert
  • Parking Assist / 360° camera (where fitted)

Not every vehicle has every feature, but many newer models include some of these systems.

Why calibration matters

ADAS makes safety decisions in milliseconds. If a camera or sensor is even slightly out of position after repairs, the system may not perform as intended.
